## What is Okta Identity Governance?


Okta Identity Governance (OIG) is Okta’s Identity Governance and
Administration (IGA) solution. The product bundle comprises three SKUs:
Okta Access Governance, Okta Workflows and Okta Lifecycle Management.

**Okta Lifecycle Management (LCM)** has been in the Okta platform for
many years and covers the end-to-end identity lifecycle. It includes
integration with identity stores (like HR systems) often called HR as a
Source (HRaaS), access entitlement policy and automation, and the
provisioning/deprovisioning integration (such as the OIN integrations)
for on-prem and cloud-based services.

**Okta Workflows** is the no-code automation platform in Okta. From an
IGA perspective, Okta Workflows can address many of the non-trivial IGA
use cases.

**Okta Access Governance** introduces entitlement management, access
requests, access certification, and governance reporting.

OIG relies on data stored in the **Okta Universal Directory** such as
users, groups and applications. It is independent of, and does not need,
single-sign on (SSO), multi-factor authentication (MFA) and other Okta
products. It does leverage platform capabilities like the System Log and
Reporting.
