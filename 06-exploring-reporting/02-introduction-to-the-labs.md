## Introduction to the Labs


This section provides an overview of the labs and the prerequisites to
run them.

### The Labs in this Section

This section has guided labs to explore different aspects of Reporting:

- **Lab 4.1 - Explore Standard OIG Reports** - a walk through of some of
  the reports available in the Okta Reporting menu

- **Lab 3.2 - Campaign Audit Reports** - a look at this new Access
  Certification feature.

The lab sections will show how the reports are accessed and run, and the
download options available.

### Prerequisites

The prerequisites for this section were covered in the earlier
[<u>Preparing Your Okta
Environment</u>](#preparing-your-okta-environment) section. If you have
run the earlier labs, you should have data that can be reported on.
Depending on your data and when you run it, some of the reports may be
empty (for example if you’ve just run a certification campaign and the
data sync has not run, the campaign reports may not show it).
