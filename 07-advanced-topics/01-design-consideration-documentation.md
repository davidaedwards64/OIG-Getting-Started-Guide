## Design Consideration Documentation


As with every Identity Governance and Administration (IGA) deployment,
you need to consider your environment and how you will design your
governance controls. How are users assigned to resources? What is the
landscape for users, applications, groups and entitlements. What is
birthright access vs. what is request-based? What processes, including
approvals need to be performed to get access? What are the access
certification requirements - what will be reviewed, by whom and how
often? What happens when access is revoked? What reporting is required
to meet your business objectives? All of this will be relevant to any
IGA deployment and we don’t have space here to go over in-depth analysis
and design.

As we have walked through the guide we have added some design
considerations when they were relevant to the relevant section.
