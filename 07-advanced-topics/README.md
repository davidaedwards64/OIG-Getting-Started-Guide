# Advanced Topics

The aim of this guide is to get you started on Okta Identity Governance.
The labs chosen and walked through above were deliberately chosen to be
entry-level to give you an understanding of the basics of the product
and how to leverage them.

This section looks at some of the more advanced capabilities. We won’t
provide guided labs on this, but merely introduce the capabilities and
where to find out more information. We also mention a bit about design.
