## Identity Governance for Other Okta Products


Okta Identity Governance functions are used in other Okta products.

### Okta Privileged Access

[<u>Okta Privileged
Access</u>](https://help.okta.com/oie/en-us/content/topics/privileged-access/pam-overview.htm)
is Okta’s Privileged Access Management (PAM) solution. It uses
[<u>access
requests</u>](https://help.okta.com/oie/en-us/content/topics/privileged-access/pam-access-request.htm)
(request types) for a just-in-time access approval mechanism, and it
uses [<u>access
certifications</u>](https://help.okta.com/oie/en-us/content/topics/privileged-access/pam-certify-service-accounts.htm)
to review access to service accounts (currently in early access).

### Govern Admin Roles in Okta

As part of the [<u>Okta Secure Identity
Commitment</u>](https://www.okta.com/secure-identity-commitment/), the
Okta platform has been hardened and this includes a new feature called
[<u>Govern Okta Admin
Roles</u>](https://help.okta.com/en-us/content/topics/security/governance-admin-roles/govern-admin-roles.htm)
which applies governance controls to who can access the Okta
administrative roles. It uses [<u>access
requests</u>](https://help.okta.com/en-us/content/topics/security/governance-admin-roles/ar-admin-roles.htm)
(conditions) for requesting access to admin roles, and [<u>access
certifications</u>](https://help.okta.com/en-us/content/topics/security/governance-admin-roles/audit-admin-roles.htm)
to review who has access to admin roles.
