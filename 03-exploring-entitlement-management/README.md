# Exploring Entitlement Management

This section explores entitlement management in OIG. If you are only
using governance to manage user assignments to Okta (or AD) groups or
applications, you can skip this section and go directly to the later
sections on access requests and access certifications (however many of
the later labs on access req quests and access certification leverage
entitlements on apps).

This section provides a more in depth view of entitlement management in
OIG then walks through bring your own entitlement management for a dummy
app and entitlement management for one of the supported apps (in this
case [<u>Salesforce.com</u>](http://salesforce.com) but it could be any
of the [<u>supported
apps</u>](https://help.okta.com/oie/en-us/content/topics/identity-governance/em/app-connectors-with-entitlements.htm)).
