## Introduction to the Labs


This section provides an overview of the labs and the prerequisites to
run them.

### The Labs in this Section

This section of the Getting Started Guide is designed to be standalone.
It does require Okta Identity Governance to be configured as per the
earlier parts of this guide.

This section has guided labs to explore different aspects of Entitlement
Management:

- **Lab 1.1 - BYO Entitlement Management** - using a dummy app in Okta,
  explore the entitlement data objects and how they could be used in
  Access Requests and Access Certification. This forms a foundation for
  the subsequent labs looking at external application integration.

- **Lab 1.2 - Entitlement Management for Salesforce.com** - having
  explored the core components of Entitlement Management, this part of
  the lab will expand to include one live application with
  entitlements - Salesforce.com. This is representative of entitlement
  management for all supported apps.

The lab sections will look at defining/importing entitlements and
defining policy. The access requests and access certifications using
entitlements are covered later in the document.

### Prerequisites

The prerequisites for this section were covered in the earlier
[<u>Preparing Your Okta
Environment</u>](#preparing-your-okta-environment) section:

- OIG enabled in your Okta org

- A dummy SCIM application for BYO entitlement management, and

- One of the supported apps for entitlement management

We will use a Building Access SCIM application for BYO entitlement
management and Salesforce.com for app entitlement management examples.
